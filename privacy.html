Manifestle Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 28, 2026

Manifestle uses Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Firebase Cloud Messaging, and a server-side AI chat proxy to provide account, social, notification, and Luna chat features.

We collect account identifiers, profile information, manifests, check-ins, shared-manifest content, device notification tokens, and chat messages only as needed to operate the app.

Shared-manifest content is visible to the participants in that shared manifest. We do not sell personal data.
